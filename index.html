    About me

    • I am currently a tenured associate professor in the School of Informatics, Xiamen University, Fujian, China and also the member of Smart DSP Lab. I received my Ph.D. degree in 2020 from Shanghai Jiao Tong University supervised by Prof. Fucai Li. During my PhD, I spent half year at Lund University, Sweden from Sep. 2019 to Feb. 2020, with Prof. Andreas Jakobsson. In 2014, I received my Bachelor’s degree from Northeastern University.

    Research Interests

    • My research interests are mainly in nonstationary signal processing, computational imaging, industrial anomaly detection, and machine learning.
